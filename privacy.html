Privacy Policy for Spinyd

At Spinyd, we are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of our users' personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect and how we use it.

Information Collection and Use

Spinyd does not require any permissions or collect any personal information from its users. We do not ask for, nor do we store, any personal data such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other identifiable information.

Event and Decision Making

Spinyd provides event planning and decision-making services without requiring any personal information from users. Users can utilize the app to organize events, make decisions, and manage tasks without the need for creating accounts or sharing personal details.

Data Security

We take the security of user data seriously. Spinyd employs industry-standard security measures to protect any non-personal information provided by users. However, since we do not collect personal information, there is no risk of unauthorized access to such data.

Third-Party Services

Spinyd does not integrate with any third-party services that may collect personal information. We do not share any data with third-party entities.

Children's Privacy

Spinyd is not directed towards children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will promptly delete it.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be indicated at the top of the policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or our practices regarding user data, please contact us at contact@spinyd.com.
